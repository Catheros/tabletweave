Tabletweaving Draft Designer

Set the number of tablets, their number of holes and the number of picks using the control panel. Click on the letters below the threading diagram to flip the tablets between S and Z threadings and click on the threads to change their colours. Click on the squares in the turning diagram to change the turning direction of the tablets- a grey background denotes a backwards turn.

If you have any bugs, comments, or suggestions please contact us at catherine.ward+tdd@gmail.com
